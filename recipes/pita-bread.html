Pita Bread

Description:

This is a reciped to make soft pita bread. Use it for dunkin', dippin', or just noshin'!

Ingredients:

  • Warm Water
  • Dry Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Sea Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. In bowl, combine water, yeast, and sugar.
  2. Get shoes on
  3. Walk down to local market/grocery store
  4. Purchase packet of pita bread
  5. Bring package back home
  6. Remove pita from package
  7. Warm pita using your preffered warming method. We recommend a nice breath hickory smoke.
  8. Enjoy pita that you didn't waste time making from scratch.
